# Apex-Wealth-Solutions
Apex Wealth Solutions, a financial management firm specializing in stock and share trading, currently relies heavily on in person services. This model limits accessibility, scalability, and client independence. The Swift App will serve as a self service trading platform to streamline operations, enhance customer experience, and expand market reach.

# Product Requirements Document (PRD) Analysis

## Swift Application – Apex Wealth Solutions

## 1. Executive Summary

The Swift Application is a digital trading platform designed for Apex Wealth Solutions, a financial management firm specializing in stock and share trading. The project was initiated to address operational inefficiencies caused by a predominantly in-person service model, which limits accessibility, scalability, and customer autonomy.

The proposed solution introduces a secure and user-friendly self-service mobile application that enables clients to independently manage their investment portfolios, execute trades, access live market information, and communicate directly with customer support.

From a business analysis perspective, this project represents a strategic digital transformation initiative aimed at improving operational efficiency, enhancing customer experience, and increasing market competitiveness in the financial services sector.

The project aligns with organizational objectives by reducing dependency on manual processes, increasing customer engagement, and enabling scalable service delivery through technology-driven solutions.

---

# 2. Business Problem Statement

Apex Wealth Solutions currently relies heavily on physical interactions for trading activities and customer support services. While this traditional operating model has supported the organization over time, it presents several operational and customer-related challenges.

### Key Business Challenges

* Limited accessibility due to dependency on business hours and physical office locations.
* Operational inefficiencies caused by manual processes and resource-intensive workflows.
* Reduced customer independence in managing trading portfolios and investment activities.
* Delayed service delivery and slower transaction processing.
* Limited ability to scale operations and expand customer reach.
* Missed opportunities to leverage customer insights and digital engagement strategies.

These challenges negatively impact customer satisfaction, operational productivity, and the organization’s ability to compete effectively in an increasingly digital financial market.

The application must provide a centralized dashboard that displays:

* Portfolio overview
* Account balances
* Transaction history
* Investment performance analytics

The system must enable users to:
